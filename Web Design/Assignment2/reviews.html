Deltarune - 9/10

Great game. Immserive story, great artwork, and an amazing soundtrack. Unlike any other game, Deltarune makes it clear that your choices don't matter. With many different battles, you have to choice to befriend or chase away anyone who gets in your way.


Animal Crossing: New Horizons - 10/10

Animal Crossing New Horizons is the newest release of the Animal Crossing franchise. The graphics for the game are amazing. The game gives you freedom to build the town of your dreams, while also meeting new residents and visting other islands.
